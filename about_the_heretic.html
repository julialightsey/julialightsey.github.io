[herətik] - One who dissents from an accepted belief or doctrine, nonconformist. Ex: Galileo was condemned as a heretic for supporting Copernicus's thesis that the earth revolves around the sun and not vice versa. Synonym: dissident - dis-si-dent - disagreeing especially with an established religious or political system, organization, or belief. A dissident, broadly defined, is a person who actively challenges an established doctrine, policy, or institution.
